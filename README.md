## Content Coverage

### Mineral Categories
- **Ferrous**: Iron, Manganese
- **Non-Ferrous**: Aluminium, Copper, Zinc, Lead, Tin, Gold, Silver
- **Strategic**: Rare Earth Elements, Lithium, Cobalt, Nickel, Tungsten, Tantalum, Antimony, Gallium, Germanium, Tellurium, Selenium, Indium
- **Energy**: Uranium, Thorium
- **Industrial**: Mica, Silicon

### Data Points for Each Mineral
- Ore types
- Uses and applications
- Global producers and reserves
- Indian producers (states, districts, specific mines)
- Indian reserves and recent discoveries
- UPSC-relevant facts and strategic importance
- Category tags (Ferrous, Strategic, Energy, etc.)

## Key Coverage

### Major Mining Regions
- **Odisha**: 95% iron reserves, bauxite (Kalahandi), manganese (Kendujhar)
- **Jharkhand**: Copper belt (Singhbhum), Uranium (Jaduguda), Lohardaga Patlands bauxite
- **Karnataka**: Gold (Kolar, Hutti), iron (Ballari, Kudremukh), bauxite
- **Rajasthan**: Zinc (Rampura Agucha - world's largest), copper (Khetri), lead
- **Chhattisgarh**: Iron (Bailadila), bauxite, first lithium mine (Korba)
- **Kerala**: Thorium (25% global reserves, world's richest monazite deposits)
- **Madhya Pradesh**: Manganese, bauxite, copper

### Strategic Minerals
- **China's Dominance**: REE (90%), Gallium (>90%), Tungsten (80%), Antimony
- **Lithium Triangle**: Argentina, Bolivia, Chile (75% reserves)
- **India's Position**: 5th largest REE deposits, 25% thorium reserves
- **Recent Discoveries**: Meghalaya lithium & bauxite, Bihar gold potential
- **Import Dependencies**: Cobalt, Nickel, Lithium, Germanium, Indium (100%)

### Famous Mines
- Rampura Agucha (world's largest zinc)
- Kolar Gold Mines (restarting after 24 years)
- Jaduguda Uranium (UCIL)
- Bailadila Iron
- Kudremukh Iron
- Khetri Copper

### Strategic Facts
- Minerals Security Partnership
- China's export restrictions (Gallium 2023, Antimony 2024)
- 2023 amendment: private sector in REE mining
- Mica classified as Major Mineral (Feb 2025)
- Thorium reactors: 99% fuel utilization vs <1% uranium
- UCIL, NALCO, HZL, CMDC roles

## Study Tips

1. Start with High Priority minerals (Iron, Aluminium, Copper, Uranium, Thorium, REE, Lithium)
2. Focus on state-wise production patterns
3. Remember strategic minerals and China's dominance
4. Learn major mine names (Rampura Agucha, Jaduguda, Kolar, Bailadila)
5. Understand import dependencies and partnerships
6. Recent discoveries important for prelims (Meghalaya, Bihar)
